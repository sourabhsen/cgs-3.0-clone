Job Description:

Medical Records Technician

The Medical Records Technician maintains the medical record component of customer charts in a long-term care facility. Included are physician order sheets, medication administration records and treatment administration records. The Medical Records Technician interacts with the other departments in the pharmacy to provide requested reports to centers on a scheduled basis.

Requirements:

  • Expert in records management
  • Enters all non-medication orders into customer specific profiles
  • Accurate maintenance of all facility forms and reporting information
  • Ability to work with directors of nursing, physicians and admin staff on resident set-up
  • Follow all applicable government regulations including HIPAA
  • Strong communicator who is able to assist internal and external customers
  • Working knowledge of multiple medical systems including CPT and Medisoft Advanced
  • Knowledge of medical and pharmacy terminology
  • Ability to work independently, meet deadlines and be flexible
  • Associates degree required
