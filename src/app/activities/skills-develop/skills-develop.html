We search hundreds of thousands of job postings to identify what the skills employers are actively looking for individual careers. The Skill Builder tool will show you the top 15 skills that are most essential for each your career goals. It also provides resources to help you build those skills and assessments to help you measure your level of proficiency.

Remember that building up your skills takes time. Make a plan to start building up your skillset using the ways outlined in stage 2.2. Use the Skill Builder tool to track your progress and re-assess your skill levels until you are at the level that you want to be.

Your task:

  • Rate your skill level for each of the top 15 skills shown
  • Explore resources to help you build the skills you need to work on
  • Take assessments for skills that have them to see how you stack up (not all skills currently have assessments)
